Welcome to the Dev Horizons

This tool provides a personalized recommendation to guide your tech career choices, analyzing your profile against data from the Stack Overflow Developer Survey 2025 and insights from the World Economic Forum.

Before you proceed, please acknowledge the following:

  • Recommendation, Not Prescription: This is a guidance tool, not a definitive career plan. The data serves as a directional guide and reflects trends up to 2025.
  • How It Works: The recommendation is calculated by a weighted algorithm that analyzes the compatibility between your profile choices and various career paths.
  • Privacy First: Your data is not saved, stored, or sent to any external server. All calculations happen entirely within your browser and are gone when you close the tab.
  • Data Source: Stack Overflow Developer Survey 2025. You can find more details at survey.stackoverflow.co/2025.
  • Licensing: Data is licensed under the Open Database License (ODbL). User contributions on Stack Overflow are licensed under CC BY-SA. © 2025 Stack Exchange Inc.
